Project Overview

As Human Resources Analyst at Walmart Global Tech, I spearheaded two major initiatives: streamlining the hiring process for a new automated tech hub and leading the implementation of a new HR platform across Walmart stores in the US and Canada. These projects involved process optimization, technology implementation, change management, and stakeholder collaboration to deliver measurable improvements in recruitment efficiency and employee experience.

Challenges Faced

  • Complex Tech Hub Launch: Establishing hiring processes for a new automated tech hub required balancing speed with quality while building employer brand in competitive technology talent market.
  • Legacy System Migration: Transitioning from legacy HR systems to modern HRIS platform across multiple locations required careful data migration, user training, and change management.
  • Multi-Location Deployment: Implementing new HR platform across US and Canada stores presented challenges with timezone coordination, regional compliance, and diverse user technical proficiency levels.
  • Compensation Competitiveness: Developing compensation strategies that balance budget constraints with market competitiveness in high-demand tech talent market.

Strategic Approach

  • ATS Implementation & Workflow Optimization: Implemented Applicant Tracking System (ATS) and optimized recruitment workflows on Workday, streamlining candidate screening, interview coordination, and offer management. This reduced time-to-hire by 23% while improving candidate experience.
  • Data-Driven Compensation Strategy: Conducted comprehensive market analysis using industry benchmarks and competitor research to develop competitive compensation packages. Achieved 95% employee satisfaction and 10% turnover reduction through strategic pay positioning.
  • Employee Engagement Programs: Implemented multi-faceted engagement strategy including employee surveys, internal communication channels, and recognition programs. This holistic approach improved employee engagement by 15% through increased connection and appreciation.
  • Enterprise HRIS Deployment: Led successful rollout of new HR platform across 2,000+ employees in US and Canada, managing data migration, user training, change communications, and post-launch support to ensure smooth transition.
  • "HelloIST" Cross-Team Collaboration: Spearheaded regular collaboration sessions fostering relationships across teams, improving internal communication effectiveness by 8% through proactive stakeholder engagement and information sharing.

Implementation Process

Project 1: Tech Hub Hiring Optimization (Months 1-6)

  • Month 1: Current state analysis, bottleneck identification, ATS requirements gathering
  • Month 2: ATS platform selection, Workday workflow design and configuration
  • Month 3: Recruiter training, candidate communication templates, interview process standardization
  • Months 4-6: Process refinement, metrics tracking, continuous optimization based on data

Project 2: Compensation Strategy Development (Ongoing)

  • Comprehensive market research across tech hubs identifying compensation benchmarks
  • Competitive analysis of top tech employers' compensation and benefits packages
  • Internal equity analysis ensuring fair pay across roles and experience levels
  • Stakeholder presentations and leadership approvals for compensation framework
  • Quarterly reviews and adjustments based on market movement and retention data

Project 3: HR Platform Implementation (Months 9-15)

  • Months 9-10: Requirements gathering, vendor evaluation, platform selection
  • Months 11-12: Data migration planning, system configuration, integration setup
  • Month 13: Pilot deployment with select locations, feedback collection
  • Month 14: Full rollout across 2,000+ employees with comprehensive training
  • Month 15: Post-launch support, issue resolution, success measurement

Project 4: Engagement & Communication Programs (Ongoing)

  • Quarterly employee engagement surveys with action planning based on insights
  • Internal communication platform implementation for transparent information sharing
  • Recognition program design celebrating achievements and reinforcing values
  • "HelloIST" sessions establishing regular touchpoints with cross-functional teams

Results & Business Impact

  • 23% Faster Time-to-Hire: ATS implementation and workflow optimization dramatically accelerated recruitment process, enabling tech hub to scale rapidly while maintaining quality standards.
  • 12% Higher Offer Acceptance Rate: Optimized candidate experience and competitive compensation positioning increased acceptance rates, reducing recruiting costs and time lost to declined offers.
  • 95% Employee Satisfaction with Compensation: Data-driven compensation strategy balanced budget constraints with market competitiveness, achieving exceptional satisfaction scores.
  • 10% Turnover Reduction: Competitive compensation combined with engagement programs significantly improved retention, reducing recruitment and training costs.
  • 15% Employee Engagement Improvement: Multi-faceted engagement strategy including surveys, communication channels, and recognition programs fostered more connected, motivated workforce.
  • Successful Platform Rollout to 2,000+ Employees: Delivered on-time, on-budget HRIS implementation across US and Canada with minimal disruption and high user adoption.
  • 8% Communication Effectiveness Improvement: "HelloIST" sessions and enhanced internal communication channels strengthened cross-team collaboration and information flow.

Key Learnings & Takeaways

  • Process Optimization Requires Technology: Implementing ATS and workflow automation was essential to achieving sustainable efficiency gains that manual process improvements alone couldn't deliver.
  • Compensation Impacts Entire Employee Lifecycle: Strategic compensation positioning affected not just recruitment success but also retention, engagement, and overall satisfaction.
  • Change Management Makes or Breaks Implementation: Success of HRIS platform rollout depended heavily on change communications, training, and post-launch support rather than just technical configuration.
  • Engagement is Multi-Dimensional: Improving employee engagement required holistic approach spanning surveys, communication, recognition, and cross-team collaboration.
  • Data-Driven Decisions Build Credibility: Using market research and analytics to inform compensation and process decisions strengthened stakeholder buy-in and demonstrated HR's strategic value.
