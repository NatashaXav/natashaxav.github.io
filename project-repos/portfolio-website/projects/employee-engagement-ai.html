Project Overview

This AI-powered tool automates the analysis of employee engagement surveys. The system generates comprehensive reports that highlight key themes, areas of concern, and positive feedback, enabling HR teams to quickly understand employee sentiment and make data-driven decisions to improve the workplace environment.
